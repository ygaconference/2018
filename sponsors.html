The University of Macau will host the “2018 Joint Annual Conference of Physical Societies in Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (YGA2018)” (粵港澳大灣區物理學會2018聯合年會) in Macao from July 26 to 29, 2018. The venue for the conference is the E4-building, and 250-300 attendees are expected. YGA2018 not only offers an excellent opportunity for scientific researchers to present their work, but also provides scientific instrument manufacturers from related fields an important stage to show their latest scientific research instruments.

We cordially invite you to sponsor events or attendees at YGA2018. We provide different sponsorship levels. We do hope you will consider joining us.
