Registration payment

● All payments have to be made in Macau dollars (MOP$).

● Cheques, bank transfers and cash payments are all allowed.

● Cash or a cheque or pay-in slip of bank transfer for the relevant exact amount for the registration fee can be made at the registration desk upon check in.

● Cheques should be made payable to: "The Physical Society of Macao".

● Electronic/telegraphic transfer should be made payable to:


Beneficiary: The Physical Society of Macao
Bank Name: Bank of China Macau Branch
Account No: 320110040511
SWIFT code: BKCHMOMX
Address: Avenida Doutor Mario Soares, Macau, China

● While transferring your registration fee, please DO state: "YGA2018 + Your FULL Name" .

● Registration will only be processed upon full payment.

● Please send your bank deposit slip copy, a copy of your valid student card or other formal identification, as well as filled conference registration form and abstract (as linked below) to the conference secretariats.

● No refund will be made once registration has been received.

● Official receipt with official chops will be issued upon request on-site.

Badges

All the conference attendees, including the speakers, must check in at the registration desk at the start of the conference, where and when a conference badge will be provided. Once the badge is received, every attendee must wear his/her official badge for the entirety of the conference in order to gain access to the sessions, and for identification purposes.
